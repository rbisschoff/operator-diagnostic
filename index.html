Which tool should you run first?

Two minutes, six questions, one clear answer on which tool to run first.

1 AI visibility
Does your direct-booking website allow AI crawlers, with no robots.txt block or firewall in the way?
Is your property described in specific traveller terms (the neighbourhood, who it suits, the practical detail) rather than generic marketing language?
2 Demand
Do you know which source markets actually drive your bookings?
Are you actively developing a high-potential inbound market, or leaving that demand to the OTAs?
3 Distribution cost
Do you know your true blended cost of distribution, including the hidden costs, not only the headline OTA commission?
Is your direct channel cheaper for the guest than your best OTA rate?
Is more than half your volume concentrated in a single OTA?
4 Unit profitability
Can you see profitability at the level of a single unit, not only the portfolio average?
Do you know which of your units lose money?
5 Guest experience
Do you capture and act on guest feedback systematically, or only read reviews as they arrive?
Do you know your return rate, the share of guests who come back?
6 Repeat guest value
When guests return, do they mostly rebook direct, or come back through the OTA you already paid to acquire them?
Have you calculated the annual revenue and commission lost when repeat guests rebook through an OTA?
